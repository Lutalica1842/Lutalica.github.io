Game Design
Enhancing
 Player Experience

Player experience (PX) refers to the emotions and overall interaction players have within the game. It includes various aspects such as storyline, character creation, visual and sound effects, and interaction design, all of which significantly influence the player's emotional engagement and satisfaction.
