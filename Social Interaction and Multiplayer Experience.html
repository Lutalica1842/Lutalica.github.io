Social Interaction and Multiplayer Experience

Social Interaction

Social interaction is crucial in multiplayer games, which can promote communication and cooperation between players and enhance the interactivity and sociality of the game. For example, in multiplayer online role-playing games (MMORPG) such as "World of Warcraft", players can team up with other players to take adventures, conduct transactions, and complete tasks together, thereby establishing a social network in the game and enhancing the social interactivity of the game. (Cole & Griffiths, 2007). In addition, social interaction can also provide opportunities for competition and cooperation between players, stimulate players' desire for challenge and cooperative spirit, and increase the fun and excitement of the game (Ferguson et al., 2012)

Multiplayer Experience

The multiplayer gaming experience is not just social interaction between players but also includes a rich and diverse gaming experience. Multiplayer games usually have richer game content and higher playability. Players can choose different game modes and characters to experience different game plots and challenges (Williams et al., 2008). In addition, multiplayer games also provide opportunities for teamwork and coordination. Players need to work closely with teammates, divide labor, and jointly complete game goals, thereby enhancing the tension and excitement of the game (Jiang et al., 2018).
