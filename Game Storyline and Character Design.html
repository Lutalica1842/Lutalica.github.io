Game Storyline and Character Design

Game Storyline

In terms of game storyline, research shows that an engaging storyline can significantly increase players’ emotional investment and game participation. For example, research by Smith et al. (2018) pointed out that the coherence and attractiveness of the storyline have an important impact on players' emotional experience and game satisfaction. In addition, research by McGonigal (2011) shows that by linking game storylines with players’ real-life situations, players’ emotional resonance and participation can be enhanced, and the game’s appeal and playability can be improved.

Character Design

In terms of character design, research also shows that a character's distinctive personality and rich background can deeply attract players and enhance their sense of identification with the game world. For example, research by Hou, Nomura, and Qian (2019) found that characters with unique personalities and distinctive characteristics in games can arouse emotional resonance among players and enhance their investment and participation in the game. In addition, the growth and development of characters is also one of the important factors that attract players. Research shows that players establish emotional connections through the growth and development of characters, thereby becoming more involved in the game (Ryan et al., 2006).
